Swing Trading Plan

Risk & Time

  • 4H time frame
  • 2% capital per trade idea
  • No trade interference
  • Set and forget

Processes

Check the current market trend, whether it's a down trend or up trend.

Wait for clear break of structure with imbalance.

Identify where liquidity has been purged.

Draw fibonacci from swing points.

Set limit at 75% fibonacci.

SL at 100% & TP at 0%.

NB : If structure doesn't meet entry requirements don't take the entry.
